Features

Track Prices

Whether you are looking to buy or just want to keep an eye on a product, track prices by adding it to your favourites list and do it with ease.

Simple & Easy UI

Simple and intuitive user interface at your finger tip, making navigation child's play.

Essential Details

Detailed view provides additional information, such as initial price when items were first added to the list, past updated prices with the aid of a graph, and percentage marked up or down of an item.

Speed

Update a few or many items in a matter of seconds.
